# Dock CLI 🐳

Welcome to **Dock CLI**! This is your friendly command-line tool to help you deploy your websites to **Dock Hosting** in seconds. No more complicated setups—just login and deploy!

## Features

* **Login quickly**: Connect to your Dock Hosting account via your browser.
* **One-command deploy**: Zip and upload your current project folder instantly.
* **Secure**: Your credentials are stored safely on your machine.
